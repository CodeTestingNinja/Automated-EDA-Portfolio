Pair Plots

A pair plot creates a grid of scatterplots for each pair of numerical variables, with histograms on the diagonal. It provides a comprehensive overview of the relationships between all numerical variables.

⚠️ Warning: This plot can be slow to generate for datasets with many numerical columns (more than 10-15).
